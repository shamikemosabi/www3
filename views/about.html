How does this work?

It's easy! stay on the home page and let the hits come to you.

You will be notify by a sound alert which you can configure in setting page

You can click the 'X' button to delete individual hit to manage your list, or just click the 'Clear All' button on the right to delete it all

Use the upvote/downvote to vote if you liked the hit or hated it

If you want to post a time on a hit, you can do it by clicking the pencil image. If the hit was really awesome and fast and you think others should do it, send the comment to the chat box!

And lastly create your own user account and join in on the chat!

Hits are sourced from Mturk Form, Mturk Grind, Mturk Crowd, Turker Nation, Mturk List, Reddit's HWTF

This started as a project and became really useful for me. As an active user at the forum I would like to thank all the contributors over at Mturk Forum Come over and visit us for great discussions, and sometimes great drama.

To support this site all I ask is to disable your ad block for this site and maybe click a few ads if you want. Thanks!
